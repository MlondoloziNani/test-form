Guidance on business size classification based on the World Bank's criteria:

  • Microenterprise: Up to 10 employees, total assets up to \$10,000, and annual sales up to \$100,000.
  • Small Enterprise: Up to 50 employees, total assets up to \$3 million, and annual sales up to \$3 million.
  • Medium Enterprise: Up to 300 employees, total assets up to \$15 million, and annual sales up to \$15 million.
  • Large Enterprise: Businesses that exceed the criteria for medium enterprises (more than 300 employees, assets over \$15 million, or annual sales exceeding \$15 million).
